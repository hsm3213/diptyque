A 60TH

anniversary

This year diptyque is celebrating its 60th anniversary. From January to December, creations and events will follow one after the other, transforming 2021 into a cavalcade of youthful creativity. Characterised by graphic design, encounters, nature, travel and curiosity, this year promises to be inventive, surprising, refreshing and playful. Get ready!
